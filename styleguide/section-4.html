4 - Modules

4.1 - Box

The "box" classes are used in cases where you need a simple bounding box.

Default styling:

Preview
.primary-box
.secundary-box
HTML
<div class="primary-box" style="width:200px; height: 50px">.primary-box</div>
<div class="secundary-box" style="width:200px; height: 50px; margin-top: 10px;">.secundary-box</div>
